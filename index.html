Hello Welcome

This web application takes your birthday details and calculates the day of the week you were born.

Depending on your gender, outputs your Akan Name

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.In Ghana, children are given their first name as a day " e.g Abenna " which corresponds to "Tuesday": The day of the week they were born.
